Company Profile

Founded in 2018, Focused on Cross-Border E-Commerce Services

Guangzhou BeiDou Connect was founded in 2018 and is an innovative company focused on comprehensive cross-border e-commerce services. Our team has over a decade of rich experience, proficient in Amazon, eBay, AliExpress, Wish, and other platforms.

We are committed to integrating premium Chinese supply chain resources and providing one-stop services for global e-commerce SMEs to help them succeed in global markets. BeiDou Connect provides system support, product supply, parcel delivery, and custom packaging services to millions of third-party platform online stores.

Looking ahead, BeiDou Connect will continue expanding its business scope to include DTC brands, e-commerce SaaS services, Print on Demand, warehousing, product R&D, artificial intelligence, big data mining, and marketing services.
